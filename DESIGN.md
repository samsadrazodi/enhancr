# Design System — Enhancr

## Identity (Phase 0 — locked, do not change)

### Palette — Darkroom Precision
- **Primary:** Charcoal-900 (`#1a1a1a`)
- **Accent:** Gold-600 (`#d4a574`)
- **Secondary:** Stone-500 (`#78716c`)
- **Background:** Cream-50 (`#faf8f3`)

No gradient blobs. Minimal, professional.

### Typography
- **Display:** Outfit (headings, navigation, buttons)
- **Body:** Inter (text, labels, small UI)

### Logo
Camera Aperture E monogram. Single-letter wordmark with aperture/light motif.
- Test at 16px minimum for icon legibility
- Appears in Navbar (left), Footer (left), landing hero

### Landing Page Messaging

**Headline:** "Enhance your photos faithfully"

**Sub-headline:** "Restore what matters. Keep what's real."

### Positioning — Three Pillars
1. **"Edits, not beautification."** No skin-lightening, no slimming, no body reshaping. Creative control and restoration over "make me look better."
2. **"One tool, many edits."** Chain edits with visible history, not one-shot flows.
3. **"Faithful restoration. Your memory stays yours."** AI repairs damage, doesn't redraw faces. Identity preserved.

## Design Constraints (Phase 1+)

- No imitation of existing photo editors (design, copy, features)
- All UI copy original
- Visual identity distinct (Darkroom Precision)
- Feature names descriptive, not imitative
- Footer disclaimer on every page (section 5.2 of AGENT_BRIEF)
- Every edited image gets "AI-edited" EXIF comment tag
- Content policy screen before first edit
- No features that remove watermarks/signatures/copyright notices
